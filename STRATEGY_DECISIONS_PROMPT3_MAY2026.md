## 8. THE ETHICS PRINCIPLE — ROUTING NEUTRALITY

This principle emerged late in the session and is not in any existing document. **It is the most important framing of AyuVinc's operating philosophy.** Add one section to the Business Plan under "Operating Principles":

---

### **Routing Neutrality: The Non-Negotiable Architecture**

AyuVinc's routing algorithm is neutral by design. No pharmacy, no lab, no clinician pays for placement or preferential routing. Orders route on performance only:

- Fulfillment rate (delivery success)
- Proximity (nearest provider)
- Speed (fastest delivery or fulfillment)
- Price (lowest cost to patient, within quality standards)

**AyuVinc earns on transaction volume, not on who pays most for visibility.**

This is not a policy that can be changed by a future board. It is the architecture — removing it breaks the product fundamentally.

**Why this matters:**

A platform that routes on merit improves clinical outcomes. A platform that routes on commission recreates the incentive failures that broke the system it claims to replace. Pay-to-play networks degrade to lowest-common-denominator pharmacies and labs. Patient outcomes suffer. Clinician trust erodes.

AyuVinc was built by a chronic patient who lived those failures. The neutrality is not negotiable. It is not a feature. It is the foundation.
